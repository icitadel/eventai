**‚ùå What to Avoid:**
1. **Dark backgrounds:** Violated EventAI style, reduced print quality
2. **Horizontal flow diagrams:** Good storytelling but wrong structure for pyramid spec
3. **Background imagery as atmosphere:** Can reduce data-ink ratio and accessibility
