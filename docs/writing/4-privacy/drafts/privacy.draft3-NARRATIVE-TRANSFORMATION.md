## Narrative Transformation Techniques Applied

### 1. **Conciseness Over Verbosity**

**Before (Draft3 - 61 words):**
> "In December 2024, Spain's data protection authority issued a **€200,000 fine** to Club Atlético Osasuna for deploying facial recognition at El Sadar Stadium and **ordered deletion of all biometric data**. The same year, the Danish DPA approved facial recognition at FC Copenhagen's stadium for football matches but **explicitly denied the identical system for concerts**."

**After (Draft4 - 31 words):**
> "December 2024: Spain fined Club Atlético Osasuna **€200,000** and ordered deletion of all biometric data. Same year, Denmark approved facial recognition at FC Copenhagen's stadium for football—but **denied the identical system for concerts**."

**Technique:** Cut "issued a fine" → "fined" (1 word). Cut "the Danish DPA approved" → "Denmark approved" (2 words). Cut "explicitly denied" → "denied" (1 word). 49% word reduction while preserving all facts.
