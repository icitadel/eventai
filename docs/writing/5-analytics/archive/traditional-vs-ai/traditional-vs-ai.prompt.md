# Traditional vs. AI-Powered Analytics Comparison

Side-by-side comparison of traditional forecasting vs AI-powered decision-making across four dimensions: Data Sources, Forecasting Timeline, Operational Adjustments, Business Outcomes. Horizontal layout, four bands stacked vertically.

## LAYOUT

**CRITICAL WHITE SPACE REQUIREMENT:**
- MAINTAIN 35-40% WHITE SPACE (measured as empty background)
- Margins: 64-80px all sides (generous breathing room)
- Element spacing: 32-48px between bands, 16-24px between related items
- DO NOT CRAM CONTENT - white space is active design element

**Structure:**
- Four horizontal bands (Data Sources, Forecasting, Adjustments, Outcomes)
- Each band split: Traditional (left) | AI-Powered (right)
- BOLD DEEP PURPLE (#6B46C1) VERTICAL DIVIDER (NOT gray or muted)
- Statistics/ROI callouts prominent in each band, electric coral color

## STYLE

**Colors (ALL three must appear prominently):**
- Deep purple (#6B46C1): VERTICAL DIVIDER, AI section headers, structural elements
- Electric coral (#FF6B6B): ROI/financial stats callouts (Data lag, ±20% error, $900K)
- Sky blue (#4299E1): Data visualization, Operational Adjustments section background (MUST be prominent)
- Midnight slate (#2D3748): Body text
- Grayscale/muted orange: Traditional approach ONLY (left side)
- Clean white background (35-40% of composition)

**Typography (MINIMUM SIZES - do not go smaller):**
- Headers: Inter bold 28-36pt (minimum 24pt)
- Body: Source Sans Pro 14-16pt (NEVER below 14pt)
- Stats: Inter 56-72pt (large, prominent)

**Design:**
- Semi-flat, rounded corners 8-12px, 2-3px outlines, minimal decoration
- FESTIVAL-SPECIFIC context: beer kegs, inventory boxes, RFID wristbands, festival stages, crowd silhouettes, weather icons, calendars
- NOT generic business stock (no corporate people, generic offices)

**Band 1 (Data Sources):**
Historical data vs Real-time + historical + external data | "Data lag: minutes (AI) vs 3-7 days (traditional)"

**Band 2 (Forecasting Timeline):**
4-8 weeks before, static "order 10,000 beers" vs 2 weeks + continuous adjustments | "±20% error (traditional) vs ±5-8% (AI)"

**Band 3 (Operational Adjustments):**
Weekly reviews, locked-in decisions vs Continuous monitoring, real-time adjustments | "Decision speed: Days vs Minutes"

**Band 4 (Business Outcomes):**
