## Four Quadrants Content

Header: Artist Rejection

## EventAI Color Palette

- Deep purple: #6B46C1 (title, quadrant borders)
- Electric coral: #FF6B6B (zero deployments stat)
- Amber: #ED8936 (opposition indicators, warning theme)
- Slate gray: #2D3748 (bottom callout background, body text)
- White: #FFFFFF (background)

## Typography

- Title: Inter Bold, 28-32pt
- Quadrant headers: Inter Bold, 18-20pt
- Hero stats (91%, 0): Inter Bold, 48-56pt
- Subtext: Source Sans Pro Regular, 14-16pt
- Bottom callout: Inter Medium, 16pt

## Visual Elements

- Festival crowd silhouettes in background (10-15% opacity, behind quadrants)
- Prohibition/X symbols for rejection theme
- NO pricing charts or system diagrams
- NO generic business icons
- Clean quadrant dividers (2-3px deep purple)
- Minimal icons (1 per quadrant maximum)

## CONCISE Density Requirements

⚠️ CRITICAL WHITE SPACE:
- Target 40%+ white space
- Generous padding inside quadrants (24-32px)
- 48-64px margins
- 32-48px spacing between quadrants

❌ DO NOT ADD:
- Pricing system flowcharts
- Algorithm diagrams
- Decorative boxes around stats
- Multiple icons per quadrant
- Gradient backgrounds

✅ FOCUS ON:
- Four clear rejection indicators
- Clean quadrant structure
- Sports success contrast
- Festival-specific visual context

- Minimum 14pt body text

## Festival Context Integration

- Subtle crowd silhouettes
