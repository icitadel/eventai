## Colors

Use ONLY these exact hex codes:
- Deep purple: #6B46C1
- Electric coral: #FF6B6B (hero numbers - PRIMARY emphasis)
- Sky blue: #4299E1
- Slate gray: #2D3748
- White: #FFFFFF

## Typography

All text should use modern sans-serif fonts:
- Title: Bold, 28-32pt (main), 18-20pt (subtitle)
- Hero numbers (50+, 74%, etc.): Bold, 56-72pt
- Labels (FESTIVALS, OPT-IN): Bold, 16-18pt
- Supporting text: Regular, 12-14pt
- Details: Regular, 12pt
- Tagline: Medium weight, 14-16pt

## Design Requirements

**Concise density:**
- Four statistics only
- Hero numbers with minimal supporting text
- Scannable in 15-30 seconds
- 40%+ white space

**Layout details:**
- Equal-sized quadrants
- 48px+ spacing between quadrants
- 48-64px margins all sides
- Center-aligned

**Background:**
- Clean white primary
- Very subtle festival crowd silhouette (10-15% opacity, bottom third)
- Crowd must NOT interfere with text readability

**Optional:**
- Small icons per quadrant (tent, checkmark, arrow, clock)
- Keep minimal (16-24px)
- Numbers are the focus, not icons

**Avoid:**
- Explanatory paragraphs
- More than four statistics
- Detailed breakdowns
- Colors outside specified palette
- Cramped layout
- Generic business imagery
