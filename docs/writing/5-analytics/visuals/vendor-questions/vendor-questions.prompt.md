# Infographic Generation Prompt: Vendor Evaluation Framework

Create a CONCISE visual showing the 5 critical questions for evaluating AI vendor claims at festivals.

## Title

Vendor Evaluation: 5 Critical Questions

## Layout Structure

Vertical list format with numbered questions, each in its own row:

```
┌────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  VENDOR EVALUATION: 5 CRITICAL        │
│  QUESTIONS FOR FESTIVAL ORGANIZERS     │
└────────────────────────────────────────┘

┌────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ 1  What festival deployments exist     │
│    with documented outcomes?           │
└────────────────────────────────────────┘

┌────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ 2  What modifications did you make     │
│    adapting from stadiums to festivals?│
└────────────────────────────────────────┘

┌────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ 3  What's the smallest event you've    │
│    successfully deployed at?           │
└────────────────────────────────────────┘

┌────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ 4  Can you provide independently       │
│    verified metrics?                   │
└────────────────────────────────────────┘

┌────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ 5  What are your failure examples and  │
│    what did you learn?                 │
└────────────────────────────────────────┘

┌────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  These questions separate evidence     │
│  from marketing hype                   │
└────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

## Visual Structure

### Each Question Row

**Number:** Large electric coral (#FF6B6B) circle with white number (48-56pt)
**Question text:** Deep purple (#6B46C1) text, Inter Bold, 16-18pt
**Background:** Clean white with subtle gray border (#E2E8F0, 2px)
**Spacing:** 16-24px between rows

### Question Formatting

Question 1:
What festival deployments exist with documented outcomes?

Question 2:
What modifications did you make adapting from stadiums to festivals?

Question 3:
What's the smallest event you've successfully deployed at?

Question 4:
Can you provide independently verified metrics?

Question 5:
What are your failure examples and what did you learn?

## Bottom Callout

Text: These questions separate evidence from marketing hype
Background: Slate gray (#2D3748)
Text color: White
Font: Inter Medium, 16pt

## EventAI Color Palette

- Deep purple: #6B46C1 (question text, border accents)
- Electric coral: #FF6B6B (question numbers)
- Sky blue: #4299E1 (optional accent on checklist icons)
- Slate gray: #2D3748 (bottom callout background)
- Light gray: #E2E8F0 (subtle question box borders)
- White: #FFFFFF (background)

## Typography

- Title: Inter Bold, 28-32pt
- Question numbers: Inter Bold, 48-56pt (white on coral circles)
- Question text: Inter Bold, 16-18pt
- Bottom callout: Inter Medium, 16pt

## Visual Elements

⚠️ CRITICAL FESTIVAL CONTEXT:
- **Illustrated festival crowd visible in background** behind the question list
- Draw diverse festival-goers in casual clothing with festival wristbands
- Include festival stage, tents, or outdoor venue elements
- Make the crowd VISIBLE and ILLUSTRATED (not just subtle silhouettes)
- Show festival operational context (NOT corporate meeting rooms)

Other elements:
- Numbered coral circles (1-5) aligned left
- Clean question boxes with subtle borders
- NO decorative icons per question (numbers are sufficient)
- Optional: Small checklist icon at top right of title
- NO generic business imagery
- NO complex diagrams

## CONCISE Density Requirements

⚠️ CRITICAL WHITE SPACE:
- Target 40%+ white space
- Generous padding inside question boxes (20-28px)
- 48-64px margins around entire visual
- 16-24px spacing between question rows

❌ DO NOT ADD:
- Icons for each question
- Decorative boxes or gradients
- Sub-bullets under questions
- Red flag/green flag indicators
- Example responses

✅ KEEP IT MINIMAL:
- 5 numbered questions only
- Clean numbered list format
- Consistent question box styling
- Simple bottom callout
- **Illustrated festival crowd in background (REQUIRED)**
- Maximum scanability

## Target Scan Time

15-30 seconds to read all 5 questions

## Accessibility

- Text contrast minimum 4.5:1 (WCAG AA)
- Electric coral circles with white numbers meet contrast standards
- Information not color-dependent (numbers + text)
- Minimum 16pt question text

## Festival Context Integration

⚠️ REQUIRED - DO NOT SKIP:
- **Illustrated festival crowd in background** - diverse people in casual festival clothing
- Festival wristbands visible on attendees
- Festival stage, tents, or outdoor venue structures
- Make crowd VISIBLE and clearly festival-themed (not faint/subtle)
- Show festival operational context (vendor evaluation happening AT a festival)

❌ NEVER INCLUDE:
- Generic business people in suits
- Corporate boardroom or office settings
- Generic tech imagery (circuit boards, binary code)
- Stadium seating (festivals use open fields/tents)
