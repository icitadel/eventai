# Before/After Comparison

Side-by-side "Before AI" vs "After AI" festival experiences across three dimensions: Discovery & Ticketing, On-Site Experience, Post-Festival. Horizontal layout, three bands stacked vertically.

## LAYOUT
- Three horizontal bands (Discovery, On-Site, Post-Festival)
- Each band split: Before AI (left) | After AI (right)
- Vertical divider (subtle line/color shift)
- Statistics/callouts prominent in each band

## STYLE

**Colors:**
- Deep purple (#6B46C1): AI elements
- Electric coral (#FF6B6B): Statistics
- Sky blue (#4299E1): Data points
- Midnight slate (#2D3748): Text
- Before AI: Muted grayscale (traditional)
- After AI: Full EventAI brand colors (enhanced)

**Typography:**
- Headers: Inter bold 24-36pt
- Body: Source Sans Pro 14-16pt
- Stats: Inter 48-72pt

**Design:**
- Semi-flat, rounded corners 8-12px, 2-3px outlines, minimal decoration
- Festival-specific imagery (stages, crowds, wristbands, mobile apps) NOT generic tech

## CONTENT

**Band 1 (Discovery):**
Manual browsing vs AI recommendations | "40-41% tickets from AI"

**Band 2 (On-Site):**
Paper maps vs real-time apps | "20% engagement with notifications"

**Band 3 (Post-Festival):**

- 40-41% ticket sales via AI
- 20% engagement (Bonnaroo notifications)
- 90% emergency alert reach (Lollapaloola)

## PRIVACY

- User control as key differentiator
