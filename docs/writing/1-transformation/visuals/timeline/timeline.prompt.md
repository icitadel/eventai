# AI Adoption Timeline: Festivals 2025-2035

Create a STANDARD-tier timeline infographic showing AI adoption in festivals from 2025 to 2035 across three distinct phases.

## Subtitle

Subtitle: *Three phases from experimental to essential*

## Layout Structure

```
[Header: Title + Subtitle]

[Timeline: Horizontal progression 2025 → 2035]
├── Phase 1 (2025-2028): Foundation Building
├── Phase 2 (2028-2032): Mainstream Integration
└── Phase 3 (2032-2035): Adaptive Ecosystems

[Dual Lines showing adoption curves]
- Major Festivals (purple line): 52.5% → 95%+
- Small Festivals (blue line): ~5% → 70-75%

[Key milestones as callout boxes at strategic points]
```

## Visual Structure

### Chart Area

**Type:** Dual-line progression chart
**Orientation:** Horizontal (left to right)
**X-Axis:** Years 2025, 2028, 2032, 2035 (labeled)
**Y-Axis:** Percentage 0% to 100% (labeled at 0%, 25%, 50%, 75%, 100%)

**Line 1 - Major Festivals:**
- Color: Deep Purple #6B46C1
- Thickness: 4-6px
- Data points: 52.5% (2025) → 67.5% (2028) → 87.5% (2032) → 95% (2035)
- Style: Solid line with circular markers at data points

**Line 2 - Small Festivals:**
- Color: Sky Blue #4299E1
- Thickness: 4-6px
- Data points: ~5% (2025) → 30% (2028) → 55% (2032) → 72.5% (2035)
- Style: Solid line with circular markers at data points

### Phase Dividers

**Vertical separator lines at:**
- 2028 (end of Phase 1)
- 2032 (end of Phase 2)
- Color: Light Cloud #F7FAFC
- Thickness: 2px
- Style: Dashed or subtle solid

### Phase Labels

**Phase 1 (2025-2028):**
- Label: "Foundation Building"
- Subtitle: "EU AI Act • Infrastructure Maturation"
- Position: Top of chart area, left third

**Phase 2 (2028-2032):**
- Label: "Mainstream Integration"
- Subtitle: "AI Becomes Baseline Expectation"
- Position: Top of chart area, middle third

**Phase 3 (2032-2035):**
- Label: "Adaptive Ecosystems"
- Subtitle: "95% Major Festival Adoption"
- Position: Top of chart area, right third

### Milestone Callout Boxes

**2025 Milestone:**
- Text: "€35M AI Act Fines"
- Icon: Shield or regulation symbol
- Position: Near 2025 baseline
- Color: Alert Amber #ED8936

**2028 Milestone:**
- Text: "$14.2B Market Size"
- Icon: Growth chart symbol
- Position: Near Phase 1/2 boundary
- Color: Warm Sunlight #F6AD55

**2035 Endpoint:**
- Text: "95%+ Major Festivals"
- Large emphasis (hero statistic)
- Color: Electric Coral #FF6B6B
- Size: 48-56pt
- Position: End of major festivals line

### Reality Check Callout

- Text: "80% report no significant ROI"
- Icon: Warning triangle
- Position: Below chart, centered
- Color: Electric Coral #FF6B6B
- Style: Small bordered box with subtle background

## Visual Elements

⚠️ CRITICAL FESTIVAL CONTEXT:
- **Illustrated festival crowd silhouette in background** (subtle, 10-20% opacity)
- Crowd should be visible at bottom or as watermark
- Festival stage icons at phase dividers
- Wristband icon for adoption metrics
- Musical note or speaker icons sparingly

**Chart Elements:**
- Circular markers at each data point (8-12px diameter)
- Subtle drop shadow on lines (2px, 10% opacity)
- Grid lines horizontal only (dashed, 1px, Light Cloud #F7FAFC)

## STANDARD Density Requirements

⚠️ CRITICAL WHITE SPACE:
- Target 30% white space composition
- Generous padding: 48-64px margins all sides
- 32-48px spacing between chart and phase labels
- 24px spacing between elements

❌ DO NOT ADD:
- Decorative borders around entire graphic
- Gradient backgrounds
- Multiple icons per milestone (one icon max)
- Excessive grid lines (horizontal only, subtle)

✅ KEEP IT BALANCED:
- Clear hierarchy (title → chart → milestones → footnotes)
- Data points directly labeled on chart where possible
- Phase information integrated into chart area, not separate boxes
- 30-60 second comprehension time

## Target Scan Time

30-60 seconds to understand:
1. Three phases of evolution
2. Major vs small festival adoption gap
3. 95%+ endpoint for major festivals
4. Key regulatory and market milestones

## Accessibility

- Text contrast minimum 4.5:1 (WCAG AA)
- Line 1 vs Line 2 distinguishable by color AND label
- Data points have both position and numerical labels
- Minimum 14pt body text, 12pt for micro-labels

## Festival Context Integration

- **Illustrated festival crowd silhouette** (subtle watermark, bottom or background)
- Festival stage icons at phase dividers (simplified outline style)
