## Event-Specific Documented Cases

**13. Bonnaroo Music Festival 2014 iBeacon Deployment**

Piloted RFID cashless in 2015, discontinued due to WiFi failures and attendee tracking anxiety

*Source: Resident Advisor, 2023*

**15. Download Festival 2015 Cashless System Failure**  
Technical failures during major UK festival deployment  
*Source: Gigwise, 2015*

**16. DoubleDutch (Mobile Event App Platform)**

Acquired by Cvent in fire sale (June 2019)  
*Source: TechCrunch, June 2019*

**17. Hopin (Virtual Events Platform)**  
Peak valuation: $7.75 billion (August 2021)  
Lost over 50% of workforce through layoffs (2022-2023)

Industry-wide abandonment 2016-2018
